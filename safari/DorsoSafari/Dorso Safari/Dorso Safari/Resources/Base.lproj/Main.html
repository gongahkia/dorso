macOS Safari Wrapper

Dorso Safari

Dorso is a local-only productivity extension. It protects selected AI chatbot sites and unlocks them after an accepted LeetCode submission on the assigned challenge.

You can turn on Dorso Safari’s extension in Safari Settings.

Dorso Safari’s extension is currently on. You can turn it off in Safari Settings.

Dorso Safari’s extension is currently off. You can turn it on in Safari Settings.

What It Does

Dorso injects a full-screen gate only on the supported chatbot domains you keep enabled. Unsupported websites are left alone.

What It Stores

This public-store build stores timer state, the staged challenge, and your selected supported-site list in extension storage. It does not send chatbot URLs or user handles to a backend.

Supported Sites

ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, DeepSeek, Copilot, Socrat, Hugging Face Chat, WriteSonic, You.com, and Jasper.
